5 amazing Bird facts

Facts

Owl is only bird which can see blue color.

Owl can see blue color and it is helpful for them when they are looking for food.

Hummingbird are the only bird who can fly backwards.

Hummingbird can fly straight up, down, backwart and forwart.They can flap their wings 20 to 80 times per second.

A group of birds is called a flock.

Birs usually form flock when they are migrating.

Ostriches have the largest eyes of any land animal.

An ostrich eye is bigger than it's brain and it is nearly 5cm.

Hoatzin chicks have claws on their wings.

Hoatzin chicks are the only bird with claws on their wings.They use their claws to climb back into trees.
